1999 PMVC Spring TT Series
Clif Bar Road-Bike Omnium - Pittsburgh Bike Oval - March 20, 1999
COMPLETE RESULTS

A surprisingly small turnout (30 riders) for a beautiful day of racing, the innaugural event of the new Pittsburgh Bicycle Oval. Races went off without a hitch; the temperature was in the upper 40s; the course was fast; and Mike Friedman showed he is about a month ahead of the rest in training. He set a really respectable record in the 5 mile individual time trial. Due to the small turnout, the original program was slightly shortened.

Spread the word: next event in two weeks (April 3, with slightly changed format).

Thanks hugely to the great officiating team: Armando Rotondi,Ed Connerley, Greg Dion, Kim Caughey, Michelle Matic, Mike Woods, Ron Kolowitz, Ron Vislosky, Stephanie Swan, and whoever else I am forgetting.
