1999 PMVC Spring TT Series
Clif Bar Road-Bike Omnium - Pittsburgh Bike Oval - April 3, 1999
COMPLETE RESULTS

A great day at the Oval, with the weather smiling, and results picking up significantly over two weeks ago. In the A race, a breakaway group of four lapped the field with one lap to go. Mike Friedman set a new record in the 5-mile individual; and a mixed group of Fraysee and Schwinn riders set a 5-mile team TT mark. Officiating was flawless. Thanks to Skip Rogers, Gregg Dion, Bram Friedman, Mr. Lucas, and the many others who ably lent their assistance.

Promoter - Oscar Swan
