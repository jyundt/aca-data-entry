   1999 PMVC Spring TT Series
   Clif Bar Road-Bike Omnium - Pittsburgh Bike Oval - April 10, 1999
   COMPLETE RESULTS

   A nice sunny day, but a nasty wind up the backstretch kept the times well below record pace for this, the last race in this series. In the A race a group of five lapped the field with five laps to go, while the B race stayed together to the end. For the third week in a row Mike Friedman posted the best time in the ITT, and Joe Papp took the A Race. No mishaps in three weeks of racing. Thanks to Skip Rogers, Gregg Dion, Bram Friedman, Dick Lucas, Ron Kolowitz, Kim Caughey, and the others who lent their assistance at the start-finish.

   Promoter - Oscar Swan
