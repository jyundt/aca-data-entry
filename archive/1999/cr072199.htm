1999 UPMC Orthopedics Cycling Record Challenge Results
Sponsored by the Pittsburgh Masters Velo Club July 24, 1999

Course records were set by Joe Papp in the 1/2 mile and in the mass start fast lap. Heat and humid weather kept results well below record times in the longer distances.

Thanks immensely to Vonn Walter and wife Natalie for helping out in the record-challenge and masters portion of the program. Thanks also to Ruud VanDijk and Skip Rodgers for their help.
